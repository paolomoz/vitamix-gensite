How do I clean my Vitamix blender?
2:34 PM

Cleaning your Vitamix is easy! Here's the quickest method:

Self-Cleaning:

  1. Fill container halfway with warm water
  2. Add a drop of dish soap
  3. Run on high for 30-60 seconds
  4. Rinse and air dry

For stubborn residue, let warm soapy water soak for a few minutes before blending.
