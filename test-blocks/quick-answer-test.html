Yes, all Vitamix blenders can make hot soup

The friction from the high-speed blades generates enough heat to bring cold ingredients to steaming hot in about 6 minutes. No stovetop needed.

The Vitamix motor spins at up to 240 mph, creating friction that heats ingredients naturally. For best results, start with room-temperature vegetables and run on high for 5-7 minutes. The soup will reach approximately 170°F (77°C), perfect for serving. Models with pre-programmed settings like the A3500 have a dedicated "Hot Soup" program that automatically adjusts speed and timing.

Quick Answer

Vitamix containers are dishwasher safe on the top rack

However, the self-cleaning feature is faster and more effective. Just add warm water and a drop of dish soap, then blend on high for 60 seconds.

For stubborn residue, try soaking the container in warm soapy water for a few minutes before running the self-clean cycle. The blade assembly can be removed for deep cleaning, but take care as the blades are extremely sharp. Never submerge the motor base in water. For best longevity, dry the container upside down on a clean towel after washing.
